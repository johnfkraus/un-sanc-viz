In accordance with paragraph 22 of the Guidelines of the Committee for the Conduct of Its Work, the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1737 (2006) concerning the Islamic Republic of Iran makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

IRi.003 AZIM AGHAJANI

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014
Date on which the narrative summary was updated: 17 December 2014

Azim Aghajani was listed on 18 April 2012 by a decision of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1737 (2006) as an individual who facilitated a breach of paragraph 5 of resolution 1747 (2007) prohibiting the export of arms and related materiel from Iran.


Additional information

Azim Aghajani was listed as “member of the IRGC-Qods Force operating under the direction of Qods Force Commander, Major General Qasem Soleimani who was designated by the UN Security Council in resolution 1747 (2007).”
