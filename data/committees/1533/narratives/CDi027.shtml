In accordance with paragraph 2 (g) of the Guidelines for the conduct of its work, the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1533 (2004) concerning the Democratic Republic of the Congo makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

CDi.027 DIEUDONNE OZIA MAZIO

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Dieudonn Ozia Mazio was listed on 1 November 2005 pursuant to the measures on arms established by paragraph 20 of resolution 1493 (2003), and extended pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1596 (2005).


Additional information

"Financial schemes with Jerome Kakwavu and FAPC and smuggling across the DRC/Uganda border, allowing supplies and cash to be made available to Kakwavu and his troops. Violation of the arms embargo, including by providing assistance to armed groups and militia referred to in paragraph 20 of Res. 1493 (2003). While president of the Fdration des entreprises congolaises (FEC) in Aru territory, Dieudonn Ozia Mazio is believed to have died in Ariwara on 23 September 2008."
