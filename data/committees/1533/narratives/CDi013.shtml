In accordance with paragraph 2 (g) of the Guidelines for the conduct of its work, the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1533 (2004) concerning the Democratic Republic of the Congo makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

CDi.013 LEODOMIR MUGARAGU

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Leodomir Mugaragu was listed on 1 December 2010 pursuant to the criteria set out in paragraph 4 of resolution 1857 (2008).


Additional information

"According to open-source and official reporting, Leodomir Mugaragu is the Chief of Staff of the Forces Combattantes Abucunguzi/Combatant Force for the Liberation of Rwanda (FOCA), the FDLR's armed wing. According to official reporting Mugaragu is a senior planner for FDLR's military operations in the eastern DRC. FDLR-FOCA Chief of Staff, in charge of administration."
