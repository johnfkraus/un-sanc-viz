In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRi.005 SANJIVAN SINGH RUPRAH

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Ruprah was listed on 16 March 2004 pursuant to paragraph 4 (a) of resolution 1521 (2003) and on 14 June 2004 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

Businessman. Former Deputy Commissioner of the Bureau of Maritime Affairs. Arms dealer in contravention of UNSC resolution 1343. Supported former President Taylor's regime in effort to destabilize Sierra Leone and gain illicit access to diamonds.
