In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.029 VIAL COMPANY

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Vial Company was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

According to a Belgian arrest warrant filed with Interpol in February 2002, Viktor Bout had power of attorney over Vial's bank accounts and used them to launder proceeds from weapons trafficking. An article in a South African academic journal reports that Viktor Bout also used Vial as the mechanism to sell aircraft to the Taliban regime in Afghanistan.
