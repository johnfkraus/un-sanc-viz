In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.001 ABIDJAN FREIGHT

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Abidjan Freight was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

Abidjan Freight was a front company set up by Sanjivan Ruprah (included on the Liberia Sanctions Committee list) to act as a freight forwarder for Viktor Bout's arms shipments to Liberia. Abidjan Freight was also used by Viktor Bout and Gus Van Kouwenhoven (also included on the Committee's assets freeze list) to conceal the exact routing and final destination of an aircraft delivering military goods to Monrovia, according to UN officials.
