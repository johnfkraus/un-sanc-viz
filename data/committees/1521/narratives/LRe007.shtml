In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.007 BUSINESS AIR SERVICES

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Business Air Services was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

Business Air Services has been identified by the "UN Panel of Experts on the Illegal Exploitation of Natural Resources and other Forms of Wealth of DR Congo" as a DRC firm in which Viktor Bout is the principal officer. The UN Panel identified it as one of several firms involved in the illegal export of diamonds, gold, copper, cobalt, timber and coltan from the DRC.
