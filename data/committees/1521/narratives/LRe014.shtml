In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.014 DHH ENTERPRISES, INC.

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

DHH Enterprises, Inc was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

Corporate registration documents list Richard Chichakli, Viktor Bout's Chief Financial Officer, as CEO, Director, and President of this company. They also indicate that he owns 100% of the capital stock. The company is located at the same address in Richardson, Texas as Richard Chichakli's accounting firm, along with all of the other firms owned or controlled by Chichakli that are being proposed for listing.
