In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.018 MOLDTRANSAVIA SRL

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Moldtransavia, SRL was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

This company is owned and managed by an admitted employee of Viktor Bout who is a longtime former agent of Air Cess. Moldtransavia's only plane is owned by Viktor Bout and is insured by a Viktor Bout company, San Air General Trading. As such, Moldstransavia is indirectly controlled by Viktor Bout. Moldstransavia has also participated in illicit arms deals for Viktor Bout and his companies. It has been involved in at least one arms shipment to Liberia.
