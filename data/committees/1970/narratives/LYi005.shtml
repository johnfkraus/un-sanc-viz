In accordance with Section 5 (g) of its Guidelines, the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1970 (2011) concerning Libya makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LYi.005 COLONEL AMID HUSAIN AL KUNI

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Al Kuni was listed on 17 March 2011 pursuant to paragraph 15 of resolution 1970 as “Governor of Ghat (South Libya)”.


Additional information

Directly involved in recruiting mercenaries.
