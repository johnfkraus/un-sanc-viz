In accordance with Section 8 of its Guidelines, the Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

KPe.001. KOREA MINING DEVELOPMENT TRADING CORPORATION


Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014
Korea Mining Development Trading Corporation was listed on 24 April 2009 “as being engaged in or providing support for, including through other illicit means, DPRK’s nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related and ballistic missile-related programmes.”

Additional information
Korea Mining Development Trading Corporation is a “primary arms dealer and main exporter of goods and equipment related to ballistic missiles and conventional weapons.”
