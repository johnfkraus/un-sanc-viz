In accordance with Section 8 of its Guidelines, the Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

KPe.002. KOREA RYONBONG GENERAL CORPORATION

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Korea Ryonbong General Corporation was listed on 24 April 2009 “as being engaged in or providing support for, including through other illicit means, DPRK’s nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related and ballistic missile-related programmes.”


Additional information

Korea Ryonbong General Corporation is a “defense conglomerate specializing in acquisition for DPRK defense industries and support to that country’s military-related sales.”
