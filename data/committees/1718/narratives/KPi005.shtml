In accordance with Section 8 of its Guidelines, the Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

KPi.005 HAN YU-RO

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Han Yu-Ro was listed on 16 July 2009 “as being engaged in or providing support for, including through other illicit means, DPRK’s nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related and ballistic missile-related programmes”.


Additional information

Han Yu-Ro is “director of Korea Ryongaksan General Trading Corporation, [and] involved in DPRK’s ballistic missile program.”
