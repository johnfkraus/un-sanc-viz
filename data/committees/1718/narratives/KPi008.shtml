In accordance with Section 8 of its Guidelines, the Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

KPi.008 RA KY'ONG-SU

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Ra Ky’ong-Su was listed on 22 January 2013 “as being engaged in or providing support for, including through other illicit means, DPRK’s nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related and ballistic missile-related programmes”.


Additional information

Ra Ky’ong-Su is a “Tanchon Commerical Bank (TCB) official, [and] in this capacity, has facilitated transactions for TCB. Tanchon was designated by the Committee in April 2009 as the main DPRK financial entity responsible for sales of conventional arms, ballistic missiles, and goods related to the assembly and manufacture of such weapons.”
