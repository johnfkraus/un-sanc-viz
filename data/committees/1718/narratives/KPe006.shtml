In accordance with Section 8 of its Guidelines, the Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

KPe.006. KOREA HYOKSIN TRADING CORPORATION

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Korea Hyoksin Trading Corporation was listed on 16 July 2009 “as being engaged in or providing support for, including through other illicit means, DPRK’s nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related and ballistic missile-related programmes.”


Additional information

Korea Hyoksin Trading Corporation is “a DPRK company based in Pyongyang that is subordinate to
Korea Ryonbong General Corporation (designated by the Committee in April 2009) and is involved in the development of WMD.”
