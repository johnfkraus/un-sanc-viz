In accordance with Section 8 of its Guidelines, the Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

KPi.011 KO CH'O'L-CHAE

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Ko Ch’o’l-Chae was listed on 7 March 2013 “as being engaged in or providing support for, including through other illicit means, DPRK’s nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related and ballistic missile-related programmes.”


Additional information

Ko Ch’o’l-Chae is the “deputy Chief Representative for the Korea Mining Development Trading Corporation, The KOMID was designated by the Committee in April 2009 and is the DPRK’s primary arms dealer and main exporter of goods and equipment related to ballistic missiles and conventional weapons.”
