In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.015 GAMBIA NEW MILLENNIUM AIR COMPANY

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Gambia New Millennium Air Company was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

The Director of this firm is Baba Jobe, who is already listed on the Liberia Sanctions Committee's assets freeze list. According to a commercial aviation database, the firm acted as a cover for Victor Bout's operations. Its one aircraft, a Russian-made passenger jet, was acquired from Centrafircan Airlines.
