In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.021 ORIENT STAR CORPORATION

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Orient Star Corporation was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

Texas State corporate records show Richard Chichakli, Viktor Bout's Chief Financial Officer, as the sole director of this firm. Chichakli identified himself in a 2004 filling as the firm's president. The company is located at the same address in Richardson, Texas as Richard Chichakli's accounting firm, along with all of the other firms owned or controlled by Chichakli that are being proposed for listing.
