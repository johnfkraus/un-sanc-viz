In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRi.022 D. BENJAMIN (JR.) TAYLOR

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Taylor was listed on 14 April 2005 pursuant to paragraph 4 (a) of resolution 1521 (2003).


Additional information

Director of Passports and Visas at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Liberia. MODEL's former Chief of Staff. He has engaged in activities that threaten to cause instability in Liberia, derail the peace process and unravel the Comprehensive Peace Agreement.
