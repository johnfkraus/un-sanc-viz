In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.010 CET AVIATION ENTERPRISE (FZE)

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

CET Aviation Enterprise (FZE) was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

Viktor Bout reorganized his holds in the UAE so that two of his older firms Centrafrican Airlines and San Air General Trading became part of a holding company called CET Aviation Enterprise. Press reports identify CET as a company controlled by Viktor Bout.
