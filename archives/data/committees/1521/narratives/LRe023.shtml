In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.023 ROCKMAN, LTD.

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Rockman Ltd was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

According to a local corporate registry, Sergei Bout is the sole owner and CEO of Rockman, Ltd., incorporated in Bulgaria. According to a commercial database report, Sergei Bout holds 100% of the voting capital of the company.
