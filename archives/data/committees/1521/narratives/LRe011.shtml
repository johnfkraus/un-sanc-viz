In accordance with paragraph 4 of resolution 2079 (2012), the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1521 (2003) concerning Liberia makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LRe.011 CHICHAKLI &ASSOCIATES, PLLC

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Chichackli & Associates, PLLC was listed on 30 November 2005 pursuant to paragraph 1 of resolution 1532 (2004).


Additional information

This company is an accounting and auditing firm. Richard Chichakli, Viktor Bout's Chief Financial Officer, is listed as the managing partner and is the only officer on record. It is located at the same address in Richardson, Texas occupied by other firms owned or controlled by Chichakli that are being proposed for listing.
