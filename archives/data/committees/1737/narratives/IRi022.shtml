In accordance with paragraph 22 of the Guidelines of the Committee for the Conduct of Its Work, the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1737 (2006) concerning the Islamic Republic of Iran makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

IRi.022 NASER MALEKI

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014
Date on which the narrative summary was updated: 17 December 2014

Naser Maleki was listed on 24 March 2007 in the Annex I of resolution 1747 (2007) as an individual involved in nuclear or ballistic missile activities.


Additional information

Naser Maleki was listed as “Head of Shahid Hemmat Industrial Group (SHIG), which is designated under resolution 1737 (2006) for its role in Iran's ballistic missile programme (designated under IRe.067). Naser Maleki is also a MODAFL official overseeing work on the Shahab-3 ballistic missile programme, Iran's long range ballistic missile currently in service.”
