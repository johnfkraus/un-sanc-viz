In accordance with Section 5 (g) of its Guidelines, the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1970 (2011) concerning Libya makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included in the sanctions list.

LYi.020 ABDELHAFIZ ZLITNI

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Zlitni was listed on 24 June 2011 pursuant to paragraph 15 of resolution 1970 paragraph 17 of resolution 1970 as “Minister for Planning and Finance in Colonel Qadhafi's Government”.


Additional information

Involved in violence against demonstrators. Secretary of the General People’s Committee for
Finance and Planning. Zltini is currently acting as temporary head of the Central Bank of Libya. He was previously National Oil Corporation Chairman. Our information suggests that he is currently engaged in trying to raise funds for the regime to replenish Central Bank reserves already spent on sustaining the current military campaign.
