In accordance with paragraph 14 of resolution 1844 (2008), the Security Council Committee pursuant to resolution 751 (1992) and 1907 (2009) concerning Somalia and Eritrea makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included on the 1844 Sanctions List.

SOi.005 FOUAD MOHAMED KHALAF

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

uad Mohamed Khalaf was listed on 12 April 2010 pursuant to paragraph 8 of resolution 1844 (2008).


Additional information

Khalaf has facilitated financial support to al-Shabaab; in May 2008 he held two fundraising events for al-Shabaab at mosques in Kismaayo, Somalia. In April 2008, Khalaf and several other individuals directed vehicle borne explosive device attacks on Ethiopian bases and Somali Transitional Federal Government elements in Mogadishu, Somalia. In May 2008, Khalaf and a group of fighters attacked and captured a police station in Mogadishu, killing and wounding several soldiers.
