In accordance with paragraph 14 of resolution 1844 (2008), the Security Council Committee pursuant to resolution 751 (1992) and 1907 (2009) concerning Somalia and Eritrea makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included on the 1844 Sanctions List.

SOi.006 BASHIR MOHAMED MAHMOUD

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Bashir Mohamed Mahamoud was listed on 12 April 2010 pursuant to paragraph 8 of resolution 1844 (2008).


Additional information

Bashir Mohamed Mahamoud is a military commander of Al-Shabaab. MAHAMOUD was also one of approximately ten members on Al-Shabaab's leadership council as of late 2008. MAHAMOUD and an associate were in charge of the 10 June 2009 mortar attack against the Somali Transitional Federal Government in Mogadishu.
