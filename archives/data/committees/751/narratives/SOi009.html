In accordance with paragraph 14 of resolution 1844 (2008), the Security Council Committee pursuant to resolution 751 (1992) and 1907 (2009) concerning Somalia and Eritrea makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals and entities included on the 1844 Sanctions List.

SOi.009 HASSAN MAHAT OMAR

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Hassan Mahat Omar was listed on 289 July 2011 pursuant to paragraph 8 of resolution 1844 (2008).


Additional information

Hassan Mahat Omar is engaging in acts that threaten the peace, security or stability of Somalia. He is an Imam and one of the leaders of Masjid-ul-Axmar, an informal Al-Shabaab affiliated centre in Nairobi. He is also involved in recruiting new members and soliciting funds for Al-Shabaab, including online at the Al-Shabaab affiliated website alqimmah.net. In addition, he has issued fatwas calling for attacks against the TFG on an Al-Shabaab chat room site.
