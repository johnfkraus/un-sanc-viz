In accordance with Section 8 of its Guidelines, the Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

KPi.006 PAEK CHANG-HO

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Paek Chang-Ho was listed on 22 January 2013 “as being engaged in or providing support for, including through other illicit means, DPRK’s nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related and ballistic missile-related programmes”.


Additional information

Paek Chang-Ho is a “senior official and head of the satellite control center of Korean Committee for Space Technology.”
