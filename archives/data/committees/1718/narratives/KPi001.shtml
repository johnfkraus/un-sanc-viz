In accordance with Section 8 of its Guidelines, the Committee established pursuant to resolution 1718 (2006) makes accessible a narrative summary of reasons for the listing for individuals, groups, undertakings and entities included in its sanctions list.

KPi.001 YUN HO-JIN

Date on which the narrative summary became available on the Committee’s website: 29 October 2014

Yun Ho-Jin was listed on 16 July 2009 “as being engaged in or providing support for, including through other illicit means, DPRK’s nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related and ballistic missile-related programmes”.


Additional information

Yun Ho-Jin is “the director of Namchongang Trading Corporation, [and] oversees the import of items needed for the [DPRK’s] uranium enrichment program. Born 13 October 1944; also known as Yun Ho-chin."
